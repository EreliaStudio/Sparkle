Detailed Description

Describes the layout of a uniform object, including its type, binding, and structure.

This struct is used to define the layout and binding information of uniform blocks within the shader programs. It specifies the uniform's name, its binding point in the shader, and a reference to the structure defining its internal layout.

Usage example: Not directly instantiated by users, used internally by Pipeline to organize uniforms.
