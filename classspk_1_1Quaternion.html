Detailed Description

Represents a quaternion for handling rotations in 3D space.

Quaternions are a system of complex numbers used in 3D computer graphics and game development for representing rotations. They offer advantages over traditional Euler angles and rotation matrices by being more compact, more numerically stable, and less susceptible to gimbal lock.
